What do you need to know about Kisfaludy in brief?

Kisfaludy was the first steamship on Lake Balaton, built on the initiative of Count István Széchenyi.

The ship first served in the Hungarian Sea between 1846 and 1887, then 127 years later, in 2015, an age-old replica was built, which is currently the only paddle-boat steamer on Lake Balaton.

Where do you find us?

Address: 8230 Balatonfüred, Vitorlás tér (Vitorlás square) (left-hand side at the end of the pier))

Our boat can be approached from the Tagore Promenade in Balatonfüred via the pier. You can see how you can find our crew even if the ship is not in the harbor in the departures section.

Further information

  • How do I get to the ship?

    The Kisfaludy paddle-boat steamer is stationed in Balatonfüred. If you arrive by car, park your vehicle near the Tagore Promenade and head towards the pier among the sailboats and look for our black-gold boat!

  • Where can I park the closest?

    In the vicinity of the harbor we recommend the use of the Zákony street parking garage, under Zákony Ferenc street 12., for a fee of 300 HUF/hour. A few minutes walk away is Huray Street parking, at Huray Street 3, where parking is 200 HUF/hour.

  • What route does the boat follow?

    Kisfaludy departs from Balatonfüred and returns there. You can admire the beauty of the Tihany Peninsula and the western bay of Lake Balaton during the one hour tour.

  • Can I take my pet too?

    Our cruise ship is dog and cat-friendly, so we are happy to have your four-legged family members too. We ask you to keep it on leash, and if it is less able to enjoy other pet companions, a muzzle is recommended.

  • How many people covered by the group discount?

    For groups over 15 people a 10% group discount is available, which can be applied at our cash desk. In case of a larger number of people, it is advisable to announce your arrival by email, so we can provide space for your group.

    (Multiple discounts cannot be combined)
  • Are security devices also from the reform era?

    Of course not! Since its construction in 2015, our ship strictly adheres to the applicable Shipping Regulations to ensure the number of required rescue equipment (even more on our ship), so as the use and quality operation of the officially required equipment.

  • Can I buy tickets online?

    Due to the variability and unpredictability of the weather conditions, you are not able to buy online tickets. You can only redeem your tickets on the spot for one of our cruises.

  • How long does a boat trip take?

    Kisfaludy takes a 1-hour trip on Balaton.

  • Is cash my only payment option?

    You can pay with cash or credit card. In case you wish to use a credit card, please, notify the cashier upfront.

  • Is there a way to book my tickets via phone?

    Scheduled cruise tickets can be purchased in person at our front desk only on the day of trip.

  • What specific programmes can I book the boat for?

    In addition to the scheduled cruises, our boat can also be hired for special events after prior arrangement (engagement, wedding ceremony, corporate event, birthday, wine dinner, etc.). Contact us and ask about the possibilities! Our crew is happy to provide you with a high quality event!
